About Me

Hi, I'm Roselle! I studied UX at the University of California, Irvine because I'm interested in the idea of making products for users, by users. In my free time, I love playing video games, cooking, baking, eating, and doodling designs. I also absolutely love dogs and own a sweet Chihuahua mix.


Originally, I was a Computer Game Science major because I was interested in making videogames. But, I quickly realized that I enjoyed creating experiences more than the technical aspects. UX is the perfect balance of creativity and design backed by research to produce an accessible application with a purpose.
