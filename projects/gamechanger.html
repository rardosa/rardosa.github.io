The Background

January 2020-June 2020

GameChanger Charity is a non-profit organization whose mission is to make an impact on hospitalized children through gaming. They recently became the official charity sponsored by DreamHack and plan to host mini-hackathons throughout the eSports event.

I joined the development team as part of my capstone project. My team and I are tasked with creating a tool that can help the organization with hosting the hackathon, with an emphasis on storytelling and continuity.

The Problem

Hackathons are a time-limited event.

Projects created in hackathons are often unfinished. They are created within a specific time frame, which leads to games that are incomplete, riddled with bugs, and most of all, unimpactful to the lives of hospitalized children.

There's no narrative.

A project's development process exists in a void. There's no story that ties together the developer's vision and the product they create.
